Applying to College?

The application process is a daunting task. In addition to highlighting your academic performance, you must reflect upon the non-academic activities and experiences that have shaped you over the last four years. Despite being important factors, GPA and SAT scores alone are not enough, and when everyone shares similar accomplishments, it can seem almost impossible to stand out.

Increase Your Chances of Admission.

While acceptance rates have continuously fallen across the nation’s top universities, ReadyEdgeGo has developed a proven process to increase a student’s chances of admission. Our dedicated admissions mentors work with each student to utilize every inch of the college application, with particular focus on college essays and supplements. The end result is an incredibly effective presentation of a student’s abilities, accomplishments, and personality.

Complete College Apps Program


  • Comprehensive guidance on essays, supplements, brag sheets, and interviews
  • Brainstorming sessions to rapidly develop essay topics
  • Checks for grammar, spelling, and messaging on all written work
  • In-person and/or virtual meetings to maximize productivity
  • 5 hours of mentorship (more hours available upon request)

Essay Review


  • Professionally written feedback for your personal statements
  • Detailed recommendations on how to improve messaging and structure
  • Follow-up 30-minute phone review
