Our Mission

ReadyEdgeGo empowers students to achieve their aspirations by providing accessible world-class mentorship services and a competitive edge in college admissions.


Our Culture

In a world where the average high school counselor is responsible for 500 students and where private counseling services cost over $30,000, it has never been more difficult for students to succeed. College acceptance rates continue to fall, high scores and grades are no longer enough, and the atmosphere at schools has become hyper-competitive.

Over the past decade, we at ReadyEdgeGo have seen these trends unfold. Our response has been to revolutionize the educational space by providing accessible support to students of all backgrounds. We believe that every student deserves more than a counselor covering 500 students, and by collaborating with public schools and communities, we have empowered countless students to reach the schools of their dreams.
