Why Hire a Mentor?

Dan Zhou

Dan knows what it takes to help students reach their highest academic and professional goals. A former teacher and coach, he has guided both domestic and international students to top universities. He also has extensive experience with the university job recruitment process, helping candidates get into companies Zynga and Google. Prior to co-founding ReadyEdgeGo, he worked at a number of startups before managing strategic partnerships at Google. Dan is a graduate of the Haas School of Business at the U.C. Berkeley, where he also studied College Admissions and Career Planning. He is a member of the Western Association for College Admission Counseling (WACAC).

Madhu Narasimhan

Madhu has assisted students across the country with their college essays and admissions strategies. His expertise includes mentorship (one-on-one admissions counseling for middle school and high school students), essay rhetoric, and the appeals process. Passionate about public service and global philanthropy, Madhu has co-founded several nonprofits and previously served as a fellow at the White House AAPI Initiative. Madhu is a graduating senior at the University of California, Los Angeles.

Venkat Narasimhan

Venkat is a renowned expert in college essay guidance and mentorship, and knows what it takes to bring out the edge in your application. Over the past decade, he has successfully guided candidates into top colleges, including the University of California, the University of Pennsylvania, and Columbia University. An alumnus of JPMorgan Chase and the financial services industry, Venkat has also played basketball at the collegiate and international levels. Venkat holds a B.A. in international relations from the University of California, Davis.

Emma Caoili

Emma has extensive experience mentoring students in a variety of settings. At U.C. Berkeley, where she earned her degree in Molecular Toxicology, Emma served as a tutor for English in Action. Upon graduating, she became an active mentor at studentmentor.org, providing personalized guidance on college and career paths for teens and young adults. In addition to her academic mentorship experience, Emma has also instructed students in ballet and piano for many years.
